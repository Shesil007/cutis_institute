Dr. S. Prasannakumar

Dr. S. Prasannakumar is a senior clinical dermatologist known for deep diagnostic expertise across pediatric and adult dermatology. His longstanding public-sector leadership and specialist practice have made him one of the most trusted dermatology clinicians in the region.

Expertise

Clinical dermatology with strong early-diagnosis focus

Qualifications

Current Role

Senior Consultant in Dermatology, Cutis Institute

Professional Summary

Dr. Prasannakumar brings a high-precision clinical approach to dermatology, with specific strength in identifying disease patterns early and tailoring treatment plans for long-term control.

His extensive institutional leadership background and broad case exposure continue to shape practical, patient-first dermatology care at Cutis Institute.

Clinical Focus Areas

  • Comprehensive dermatology consultation for children and adults.
  • Early-stage diagnosis of complex and chronic skin conditions.
  • Structured management of inflammatory and infectious dermatoses.
  • Longitudinal skin care planning for recurrent disease patterns.

Career Highlights

  • Former Head of Department of Dermatology, Government General Hospital, Calicut.
  • Former Superintendent, Government Leprosy Hospital, Calicut.
  • Recognized senior dermatology specialist serving Malabar patients for years.
  • Known for calm, compassionate, and detail-driven clinical care.

Care Philosophy

Every case begins with careful clinical evaluation, not assumptions. Dr. Prasannakumar emphasizes early detection, clear communication, and practical treatment planning that patients can follow consistently.

His approach balances evidence-based medicine with real-world day-to-day usability, especially for patients with long-standing or recurring skin conditions.
