Clinical Head & Senior Consultant in Clinical, Surgical & Aesthetic Dermatology

Dr. T. Salim

MD, DNB, MNAMS

Dr. T. Salim is a senior dermatologist with more than 25 years of clinical experience in advanced dermatology, dermatosurgery, pigment science, laser platforms, and aesthetic dermatology. He leads high-complexity care pathways at Cutis and has contributed extensively to clinical education and research.

Experience

25+ years in clinical, surgical and aesthetic dermatology

Key Focus

Pigment disorders, lasers, dermatosurgery, vitiligo procedures

Current Position

Clinical Head & Senior Consultant, Cutis Institute

Professional Summary

Dr. Salim combines medical dermatology, procedural dermatology, and aesthetic medicine in a single integrated practice model. His work has strongly influenced pigment-cell therapies, scar-focused procedures, and laser-based skin correction protocols in the region.

In addition to full-time clinical leadership, he has contributed to academic mentoring, conference teaching, and skill transfer for practicing dermatologists across India and international platforms.

Clinical Expertise

  • Advanced clinical dermatology and difficult-case diagnosis.
  • Laser dermatology for scars, pigmentation, rejuvenation, and texture correction.
  • Dermatosurgical planning and procedure-led skin restoration.
  • Pigment-cell and vitiligo-focused intervention pathways.
  • Long-term evidence-based follow-up protocols for sustained outcomes.

Leadership Highlights

  • Adjunct Faculty, Department of Dermatology, Kasturba Medical College, Manipal.
  • International Travelling Mentor for the American Society of Dermatologic Surgery (since 2012).
  • National President, Association of Cutaneous Surgeons of India.
  • Board member, Dermatologic and Aesthetic Surgery International League (DASIL).
  • Former Assistant Professor in Dermatology, Calicut Medical College.

Major Contributions & Recognition

  • Pioneered early melanocyte transplantation workflows for leucoderma care at Cutis.
  • Trained in lasers, aesthetic dermatology, and dermatosurgery through multiple international programs.
  • Regular faculty and invited speaker at specialist dermatology and dermatosurgery platforms.
  • Recognized with multiple professional honors, including the ASDS ITMP Outstanding Achievement Award (2020).
