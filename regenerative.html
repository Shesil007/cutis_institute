Regenerative Dermatology

Regenerative Medicine

The most powerful healer is the one already within you. Regenerative Dermatology is about activating that biology — precisely, safely, and with clinical intent.

Unlike conventional treatments that manage symptoms at the surface, Regenerative Dermatology works at a cellular level. By harnessing the body's own growth factors, platelet-rich plasma, exosome signalling, and stem cell-conditioned media, we stimulate the skin's intrinsic repair mechanisms to restore health, texture, and vitality from within. This is not cosmetic care — it is biological restoration guided by clinical science.

Who This Is For

Regenerative therapies are suited to patients seeking deep skin renewal, accelerated healing, hair restoration, or improvement in skin quality that standard treatments have not fully addressed. They are also used as powerful add-on therapies following laser or energy-based procedures to enhance outcomes and reduce recovery time.

Treatments Available

Each modality is selected based on your specific skin concern, health profile, and treatment goals.

◈
Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP)

Drawn from your own blood and processed to concentrate growth factors. Injected or applied topically to stimulate collagen, elastin, and tissue repair for skin rejuvenation and hair restoration.

◈
Exosome Therapy

Advanced extracellular vesicles that act as biological messengers. They deliver growth factors and signalling proteins to damaged cells, accelerating repair, reducing inflammation, and promoting deep skin renewal.

◈
Stem Cell Conditioned Media

A next-generation regenerative therapy using the secretome of stem cells — concentrated proteins and growth factors that instruct the skin to heal, regenerate, and rebuild collagen.

◈
Microneedling with Regenerative Agents

Precision micro-channels created in the skin allow regenerative substances — PRP, exosomes, or stem cell media — to penetrate at depth for amplified, long-lasting results.

◈
Growth Factor Mesotherapy

Direct microinjection of growth factors and regenerative compounds into the scalp or skin. Particularly effective for early-stage hair thinning and overall skin vitality restoration.

◈
Post-Procedure Regenerative Support

Structured regenerative add-on protocols following laser, RF, or chemical resurfacing treatments — accelerating healing time and maximising the outcomes of your primary procedure.
