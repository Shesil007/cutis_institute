Capeli Clinique

Hair & Scalp Care

Hair loss is rarely straightforward. Lasting results begin with the right diagnosis — and that is exactly where Capeli Clinique starts.

Our specialist hair and scalp center combines trichology expertise with advanced dermoscopic imaging to understand the root cause of hair thinning, loss, or scalp conditions. Every patient receives a detailed clinical assessment before a customized hair restoration pathway is defined — because no two cases of hair loss are the same.

Who This Is For

Anyone experiencing hair thinning, diffuse hair loss, patchy baldness, scalp inflammation, or persistent dandruff will benefit from Capeli's specialist evaluation. We see both male and female patients across all age groups.

Our Approach

We combine clinical history, blood investigations, and dermoscopic scalp analysis to classify the type and stage of hair loss accurately. Treatment plans are then structured — incorporating medical management, in-clinic procedures, and maintenance strategies tailored to your hair and scalp type.

Treatments Available

◈
Trichoscopy / Dermoscopy

High-resolution scalp analysis to accurately classify hair loss type and stage.

◈
PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma)

◈

◈
