Complete Overview

Cutis Institute of Dermatology & Aesthetic Sciences in Calicut, India, is one of India's largest standalone skin institutes. Located in a 21,000-square-foot facility, Cutis offers world-class care for all skin needs in Clinical, Cosmetic, and Surgical Dermatology, along with plastic and reconstructive surgery.

Our dermatologists and plastic surgeons combine cutting-edge technology with compassionate care to deliver transformative results in skin health, aesthetic medicine, hair transplant, and plastic and reconstructive surgery. Experience trusted, expert-led care at Cutis Institute in Calicut.

Our team of board-certified dermatologists and plastic surgeons, clinical assistants, and compassionate staff are dedicated to delivering personalized care in a comfortable and welcoming environment. Our commitment lies in offering comprehensive and advanced therapies that combine medical expertise with procedural interventions. We understand that each patient is unique, which is why we prioritize individualized treatment plans to achieve optimal results.

Beyond our commitment to advanced technology, we prioritize patient education and transparency. Our team takes the time to thoroughly explain procedures, discuss expectations, and address any concerns you may have, ensuring you feel confident and informed throughout your journey with us.

Mission

To provide and promote world class scientific skin care with the support of the state of the art technology updations in Dermatology, Laser medicine, Cosmetology and Plastic Surgery. And to impart top notch training in advanced dermatology techniques to skincare professionals and residents.

Vision

To be the premier global destination for the treatment of all skin conditions and disorders which is backed by the finest group of competent and committed Dermatologists and Plastic Surgeons with diversified specialties to cover all aspects of medical, surgical and cosmetic dermatology and plastic surgery.
