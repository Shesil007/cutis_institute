Visiting Consultant in Dermatology & Hair Transplant Surgery

Dr. Amit Kerure

MBBS, MD, DVL

Dr. Amit Kerure is a dermatologist and hair transplant surgeon associated with Cutis Institute as a Visiting Consultant. He focuses on evidence-based skin and hair care with strong clinical emphasis on trichology, procedural dermatology, and natural-looking restoration outcomes.

Experience

10+ years in dermatology and hair restoration

Key Focus

Hair transplant surgery, trichology, and procedural dermatology

Current Role

Visiting Consultant, Cutis Institute

Professional Summary

Public clinical profiles for Dr. Amit Kerure highlight over 10 years of dermatology practice and more than 1,500 successful hair transplant procedures. His treatment model combines detailed diagnosis, realistic counseling, and stage-wise procedure planning.

At Cutis Institute, he contributes as a visiting specialist for complex hair loss assessments, hair restoration strategy, and integrated dermatology pathways requiring procedural precision.

Clinical Focus Areas

  • Hair transplant planning and execution with natural hairline design principles.
  • Diagnosis and treatment of patterned and non-patterned hair loss conditions.
  • Trichology-led maintenance protocols after transplant procedures.
  • Procedural dermatology for acne scars, pigmentation, and texture correction.
  • Laser and regenerative support therapies as part of comprehensive care.

Academic & Research Work

  • Co-authored peer-reviewed dermatology and trichology publications indexed in major medical databases.
  • Published work on complications and management strategies in hair transplant practice.
  • Contributed clinical research in psychodermatology and acne-linked stress pathways.
  • Involved in collaborative clinical writing on procedural dermatology case management.

Selected Publication Topics

  • Donor Area Acute Effluvium Following Follicular Unit Extraction (Journal of Cutaneous and Aesthetic Surgery, 2020).
