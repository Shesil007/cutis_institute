CutiSculpt

Body Sculpting

CutiSculpt is where science meets body confidence. A medically guided center for face and body contouring — precise, non-surgical, and clinically credible.

Some concerns are resistant to diet and exercise. Stubborn fat deposits, loose skin after weight loss, facial volume loss, and body laxity require a more targeted clinical approach. CutiSculpt offers a comprehensive range of the most advanced non-surgical body and face contouring technologies — each selected, combined, and administered under expert supervision to deliver visible, measurable, and lasting results without surgery or significant downtime.

Who This Is For

CutiSculpt is designed for patients who are at or close to their ideal body weight but have localised concerns — stubborn fat pockets, loose skin, reduced facial volume, cellulite, or overall body laxity — that have not responded to lifestyle changes alone. It is also an excellent option for patients seeking post-weight-loss body tightening or facial rejuvenation without surgery.

Our Approach

Every CutiSculpt patient begins with a clinical body assessment — examining skin quality, fat distribution, tissue laxity, and overall aesthetic goals. Our specialists combine the most appropriate technologies from fat reduction, muscle toning, and skin tightening to create a structured, multi-modal treatment plan. We do not apply single-device solutions to complex concerns. We build a programme tailored to your body and your goals.

Technologies We Use

CutiSculpt combines the most effective non-surgical body and face contouring technologies available, selected and combined based on your individual assessment.

◈
ULTRAcel Q+ HIFU

High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound for non-surgical face and body lifting, skin tightening, and selective fat cell disruption. One of the most effective non-invasive contouring systems available.

◈
Endymed 3DEEP Radiofrequency

Endymed — multi-source phase-controlled RF technology for deep tissue skin tightening, collagen remodelling, and body contouring across face and body areas.

◈
Cryolipolysis (Fat Freezing)

FDA-cleared controlled cooling technology that permanently destroys fat cells in localised areas. The body naturally processes and eliminates the affected cells over 8–12 weeks.

◈
Injection Lipolysis (Aqualyx)

Minimally invasive micro-injections that dissolve and eliminate stubborn localised fat deposits in targeted areas such as the chin, arms, inner thighs, and abdomen.

◈
Body Mesotherapy

Direct microinjection of active compounds into the dermis to improve skin texture, hydration, and localised fat management — particularly effective for cellulite-prone areas.

◈
Alma Accent Prime

Combines ultrasound and RF energy for effective body shaping, cellulite reduction, and skin tightening across larger body areas with comfortable, controlled treatment delivery.
