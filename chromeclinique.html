Chromeclinique

Pigment Correction

Skin tone is deeply personal. When pigmentation becomes uneven, unpredictable, or difficult to manage, it takes more than a routine treatment — it takes a specialist.

Chromeclinique is Cutis Institute's dedicated center for the full spectrum of pigment-related conditions. From post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation and melasma to vitiligo and rare depigmentation disorders, our specialists approach each case with clinical precision and a customized treatment plan. We combine advanced diagnostic tools with evidence-based laser and light therapies to deliver targeted, lasting results.

15+ Years treating pigmentation
FDA Approved devices used
100% Customised treatment plans

Who This Is For

Chromeclinique is designed for patients who are dealing with persistent or complex pigmentary concerns that have not responded well to general skincare or standard treatments. From vitiligo and birthmarks to moles, melanocytic lesions, and hyperpigmentation disorders, our specialists assess each concern at a deeper clinical level before planning treatment.

Vitiligo Birthmarks Melasma Hyperpigmentation Freckles & Lentigines Moles Melanoma & Skin Cancers

Our Approach

We begin with a thorough clinical assessment and Wood's lamp or dermoscopic analysis to classify the type and depth of pigmentation. From there, a structured treatment plan is built by integrating medical therapies, procedural interventions, and the most appropriate laser and light systems specific to your skin type and pigment concern.

✦Expert-led clinical diagnosis
✦Precision laser therapy
✦Tailored, monitored treatment plans
✦Sun protection & maintenance guidance

Specialized Vitiligo Treatment Centre

We specialize in vitiligo treatment, utilizing a range of cutting-edge therapies tailored to address this complex condition. Our facilities include phototherapy, Excimer lasers, and targeted light-based systems that play crucial roles in managing vitiligo by stimulating repigmentation.

At Chromeclinique, we take pride in our consultants who are pioneers in melanocyte cell transplantation. Their expertise has not only led to successful treatments for numerous patients but has also involved training dermatologists globally in this groundbreaking technique.

Advanced Technologies for Hyperpigmentation

For hyperpigmentation disorders, our center offers a diverse array of technologies and treatments. This includes Q-switched Nd:YAG lasers, pulse light systems, fractional lasers, fractional radiofrequency technologies, chemical peels, and other procedural treatments tailored to specific pigmentation concerns for optimal outcomes.

Our approach involves the integration of medical therapies and procedural interventions. This combined strategy ensures a holistic and effective treatment plan that caters to individual patient needs and maximizes results.

Commitment to Advancement and Education

At Chromeclinique, our commitment extends beyond patient care. We prioritize continuous advancement in pigmentary disorder treatments and actively contribute to educating dermatologists worldwide through our consultants' expertise and training programs.
