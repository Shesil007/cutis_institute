Eraze Scar Center

Scar Restoration

Scars carry stories. Our role is to help rewrite them — with clinical expertise, advanced technology, and a treatment plan built around you.

The Eraze Scar Center specializes in the comprehensive management of scars across all types and severities. Whether it is acne scarring that has affected your confidence for years, a post-surgical mark, or a keloid that has resisted standard care, our specialists create a structured revision pathway designed for lasting results.

Who This Is For

Patients with any type of scar — from mild post-acne marks to severe hypertrophic keloids — will find structured, expert care at Eraze. If a scar has not responded to standard treatment, we can design a specialist revision plan.

Acne ScarsKeloidsBurn ScarsSurgical ScarsSkin TextureHypertrophic Scars

Our Approach

We begin with a clinical scar classification and photography-based assessment. Treatment is then structured across multiple modalities — combining fractional laser resurfacing, radiofrequency microneedling, chemical reconstruction, and intralesional therapies as appropriate for your specific scar type.
