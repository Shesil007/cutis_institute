Luminique

Light Therapy

Some skin conditions require the precision of light. Luminique brings together cutting-edge phototherapy technologies under expert clinical supervision.

From whole-body UVB phototherapy for psoriasis and vitiligo to targeted excimer light for isolated patches, Luminique offers a structured, measured, and closely monitored therapeutic environment. Every treatment course is designed around clinical response, not convenience.

Who This Is For

Luminique is for patients managing chronic or recurrent skin conditions that require structured phototherapy. It is particularly effective for vitiligo, psoriasis, eczema, and other inflammatory skin conditions that need precise light-based management.

Our Approach

Treatment begins with clinical evaluation to determine the most appropriate phototherapy modality and dosimetry schedule. Sessions are carefully monitored and adjusted based on skin response, ensuring maximum therapeutic benefit with minimal risk.
