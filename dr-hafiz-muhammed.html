Dr. Hafiz Muhammed Koyappathody

Dr. Hafiz Muhammed leads cosmetic surgery services with strong grounding in plastic and reconstructive surgery. His work combines surgical precision, structured planning, and patient-specific aesthetic design.

Current Role

Qualifications

Core Domain

Professional Summary

Dr. Hafiz Muhammed is a senior plastic surgeon with advanced focus on aesthetic outcomes and reconstructive precision. He is known for balancing functional restoration with proportionate, natural cosmetic results.

His practice includes detailed pre-surgical counseling, realistic outcome planning, and close postoperative follow-up for sustained quality of care.

Advanced Training

  • Core training through Calicut Medical College.
  • Additional specialty exposure at Nizams Institute, Hyderabad.
  • Advanced surgical training at Amrita Institute, Kochi.
  • Fellowship in Reconstructive Microsurgery from Amrita Institute, Kochi.

Clinical Focus

  • Aesthetic surgery planning and proportion-driven correction.
  • Plastic and reconstructive surgical restoration.
  • Procedure design with emphasis on symmetry and natural contour.
  • Personalized pathways for cosmetic concerns and recovery goals.

Practice Philosophy

Every surgical plan is individualized after face/body analysis, concern prioritization, and risk-benefit discussion. Dr. Hafiz emphasizes clarity before intervention and long-term follow-through after procedures.

His care model is built around safe technique, predictable recovery, and outcomes that remain aligned with each patient’s natural anatomy.
