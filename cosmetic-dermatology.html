Cosmetic Dermatology

Enhancing your natural beauty

Advanced Aesthetic and Rejuvenation Care

We specialize in advanced anti-aging, skin rejuvenation, and cosmetic treatments tailored for every skin type. Our comprehensive programs combine cosmeceuticals, chemical peels, photo facials, and cutting-edge light-based therapies to restore youthful, radiant skin. From reversing signs of aging to achieving a flawless glow, our gentle, science-backed treatments bring out the very best version of you.

Anti-Aging Clinic

Our specialized anti-aging treatments offer both surgical and non-surgical solutions to target wrinkles, loss of volume, and skin laxity. We utilize advanced procedures like Botox, dermal fillers, thread lifts, and radio-frequency skin tightening to restore facial contours and gently lift the skin.

For complete skin rejuvenation, we offer laser therapies, oxypeels, and collagen-regenerating treatments such as Exosome therapy, PRP, and stem cell therapy, ensuring visible and lasting results for a radiant complexion.

Pigment Clinic

Our state-of-the-art pigment clinic helps visibly reduce deep and resistant discoloration. We treat various forms of hyperpigmentation—including freckles, melasma, periorbital pigmentation, and tattoos—using medical, physical, and surgical modalities such as Q-Switched Nd:YAG Laser, Intense Pulsed Light (IPL), and Excimer Light.

Acne Clinic

We treat active acne and scars in a safe, medically appropriate manner to heal existing lesions and prevent new ones from forming. Our approach combines medical and light-based treatments, including chemical peels, microneedling radiofrequency, fractional lasers, and photodynamic therapy to minimize scarring and restore skin clarity.

Comprehensive Hair Clinic

We provide evidence-based treatments for a wide range of hair and scalp concerns. Our hair restoration services include Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) therapy, Mesotherapy, Growth Factor Concentrate (GFC), and advanced stem cell therapies to stimulate natural hair regrowth.

For permanent hair restoration, our expert surgeons perform Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE) and Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT) to deliver natural-looking results with precision and minimal downtime.

Nail Clinic

Our dedicated nail care clinic diagnoses and treats a variety of nail disorders, including fungal infections, nail psoriasis, and trauma. We utilize the latest advancements such as FDA-approved laser therapy, specialized medications, and nail plate restoration techniques to ensure the long-term health and aesthetics of your nails.
