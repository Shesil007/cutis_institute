Welcome to Cutis Institute

Cutis Institute of Dermatology & Aesthetic Sciences in Calicut, India is a leading skin clinic and skin hospital specializing in skincare, cosmetology, and plastic surgery.

Located in a 20,000 square feet standalone building, we provide world-class care in Clinical, Cosmetic, and Surgical Dermatology through an expert team of specialists.
